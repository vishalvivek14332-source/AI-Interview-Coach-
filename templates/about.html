Project Purpose
Why this platform was engineered
🎯

Democratize Interview Prep

Simulating live technical interviews is often expensive or unavailable. This tool provides instant, comprehensive practice sessions completely free of charge.

🔒

Private-First Framework

No telemetry, third-party analytics, or tracking platforms. All records, audio transcript summaries, and feedback metrics reside on your own local SQLite database.

📈

Structured Feedback

Uses standardized professional metrics (relevance, accuracy, confidence) to pinpoint specific skills and knowledge areas for improvement.

Core Flow Architecture
End-to-End system processing walkthrough
1

👤 Candidate Onboarding

The candidate selects a job role and experience level. A session-specific instance is initiated in the local SQLite database.

2

🤖 Dynamic Questioning

The system passes session history to Google Gemini to build specialized questions tailored to the candidate's experience level and prior performance.

3

🎙️ Speech Transcript Capture

The candidate responds. Real-time audio parsing translates speech via the Web Speech API before transmitting transcripts to the server.

4

🧠 Multidimensional Grading

The response is sent to Gemini for grading across five key dimensions. Feedback is immediately displayed to the candidate.

5

🏆 Document Compilation

On completion, average scores are compiled. Professional PDF reports and certificates are generated using ReportLab, ready to download or email.
