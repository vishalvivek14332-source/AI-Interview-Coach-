About AI Interview Coach

AI Interview Coach is an intelligent, fully local interview preparation platform powered by the Anthropic Claude API. It generates role-specific questions across technical, HR, and behavioral categories, evaluates your answers using NLP, and provides actionable feedback — all in real time.

Built with Flask, SQLite, and vanilla JavaScript, the platform requires no external subscriptions. Your interview data is stored privately on your own machine. The goal is simple: help you walk into any interview with confidence.
